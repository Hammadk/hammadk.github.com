LookRook: A Rails app for finding the best stylists in your hometown

LookRook is a Ruby on Rails app, that lets people find the best stylist in their hometown.

This idea for this app is really simple; make it super easy for stylists to upload pictures of their work online (and establish their portfolio). On the other hand, make it really easy for users to find the best stylists in their home town, based on the photos uploaded by the stylists.
